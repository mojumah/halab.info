The City of Aleppo

A website with photos and information about the city of Aleppo.

Some facts

Aleppo is the second largest city in Syria. Famous for it's citadel, the city is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world; it may have been inhabited since the 6th millennium BC. From The book The Times Complete History of the World "the citadel was mostly constructed by AZ-Zahir Ghazi, it is one of the greatest fortresses in the north Syria region. The citadel embodied the most recent technologies of its time such as bend entrances and box-machicolations to deter attack"
